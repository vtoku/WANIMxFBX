Embed recorded blendshape animation in the exported FBX

The FBX can now carry the captured ARKit face animation, not just the body:

- preview/face.ts: loadFaceMeshData() extracts the facecap head geometry +
  52 morph-target deltas (world-baked) for export.
- clip.ts: resample() now also resamples the face weight tracks.
- fbx/animationFbx.ts: opts.face embeds the head as a Mesh Geometry +
  BlendShape/BlendShapeChannel deformers + Shape geometries (per-vertex
  deltas), parented to the Head bone, with DeformPercent curves driven by the
  recorded weights. Mesh baked into Head-local space (scale ~22cm, 180deg Y,
  lift 9cm). Definitions counts now derived from the emitted objects.
- main.ts: "Face blendshapes" toggle (default on) builds the channels by
  mapping ARKit names to the facecap morphs.

Verified headlessly (npm run faceFbxCheck): the emitted binary FBX round-trips
through FBXLoader as 1 morph mesh with 48 morph targets and 104 clip tracks
(56 bone + 48 morph). Browser download verified, no console errors.
